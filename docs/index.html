How it works

Cheap math finds the candidates; a local vision model rules on the hard calls. Each stage runs on demand and you review the results in your browser.

Stage 1

Exact

Byte-identical files, matched instantly from Drive's own checksums — no image data needed.

Stage 2

Near

Re-encoded, resized, or lightly edited copies, caught with perceptual hashing. Colorway variants get flagged.

Stage 3

Similar

The same scene from a different shot, clustered with CLIP image embeddings and vector search.

Stage 4

Adjudicate

Borderline pairs are sent to your local Ollama vision model for a same / near / variant / different verdict.

Stage 5

Tag & review

Optional brand tagging on the keepers, then keep/trash review with a one-click CSV export.

Private by design

Your photos never leave your computer, and doppel never touches Drive.

●Read-only, always

doppel requests only the drive.readonly scope and never trashes, moves, or renames anything. The output is a review list and a CSV — you decide what to delete.

●Runs on localhost

The web app binds to 127.0.0.1 only. No account, no cloud, no deployment — it is served by your own machine and reachable only from it.

●Your model, your metal

Duplicate detection and the vision model both run locally — CLIP embeddings on your GPU, adjudication through your own Ollama instance. No third-party API sees your images.

●Just thumbnails

Detection works on cached 512px thumbnails, not your originals. Full-resolution fetch is used only if you opt into brand tagging.

Get started

doppel is a local app, so you run it from the repo. Two commands and you're up.

# clone and launch
git clone https://github.com/holyscotsman/Doppel.git
cd Doppel
make setup   # installs Python 3.12 + dependencies via uv
make run     # serves http://127.0.0.1:8000

Then open http://127.0.0.1:8000/setup — the built-in wizard walks you through the three connections:

Connect Google Drive

Upload an OAuth client file (a free "Desktop app" credential from the Google Cloud Console) and authorize in your browser. Read-only, one time.

Point at your local LLM

Enter your Ollama address and pick an installed vision model (gemma3, qwen3-vl) from the dropdown. doppel tests the connection before saving.

Choose what to scan

Scan your whole Drive, or paste a folder link to scope it to one folder and its subfolders.

Why local? doppel works with things that live on your machine — your Ollama model, your compute, and photos you'd rather not route through anyone else's server. So the app runs on your computer and this page just gets you there; the actual Drive and Ollama connections happen in the local /setup wizard, not on this website.
